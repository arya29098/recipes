Carbonara

Description

Quick and cheap carbonara recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 16 oz
  • Guanciale - 100g
  • Parmiggiano-Regiano - 100g
  • Egg - 4
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare egg & cheese mix by mixing 4 eggs and finely grated Parmiggiano-Regiano in a bowl
  2. Put in Guanciale in cold pan, and heat up in low heat for 15 minutes until the fat renders
  3. Cook the pasta in water. Follow the instructions or until al dente
  4. Turn off the heat on the pan, and add the pasta to the pan and coat pasta in Guanciale fat
  5. Pour in the egg & cheese mix
  6. Serve hot
